What we do

DRV Educational Solutions is an educational consulting firm that conducts Summary of Finance (SOF) and PEIMS data reviews. We are devoted to training and helping school districts based on your district’s needs. Whether it is finding unrecovered money or a question about PEIMS we've got you covered.

Who we are

We are a group of individuals dedicated to bringing innovative solutions to the education marketplace. With our collective years of experience, we have a vast amount of knowledge to share with your district.

Interested in learning more? We'd love to chat with you.

 

Our Services

PEIMS Data Review

This service was developed in response to the need for increased PEIMS data accuracy and how that data accuracy relates to the potential for increased revenue opportunities. This accuracy becomes even more important in an environment of lower state funding and increased accountability for schools across the state. This review is intended to help districts get a better understanding of where weaknesses may exist in the accounting attendance procedures, processes and practices.

Personnel Staffing Review

For those districts who may need to lose some weight (staffing) or are on a diet, this crowd favorite was created by one of the most famous school finance chefs in the state. This service will help your district run that extra mile.

Recovery of Funds

If a PEIMS Data Review indicates the possibility of funds from prior years that can be recovered, then our team will assist in helping with the recovery. Recovery of prior year funds will be billed at 15% of the amount recovered not to exceed a certain amount.

Training Smorgasbord

Our team offers trainings that are customized to your needs and relevant to the current environment facing school districts. We can provide half day or all day training sessions. With a cost per person as low as $75.00, your district will save money and valuable time for your staff. So don’t travel, just train! Various Training Topics:

  • PEIMS 101 Awareness Training
  • PEIMS and Cross Campus Collaboration Training
  • Summary of Finance Training for Business Managers and CFOs
  • SAAH Student Accounting Handbook / PEIMS Trainings
  • Board training and presentations on various topics
  • Staff Development Trainings for Service Centers

Real Time Agents

This subscription based service uses new video chatting technology, and brings some of the greatest field agents to your doorstep in the areas of SOF and PEIMS, cutting all of the travel expenses.
